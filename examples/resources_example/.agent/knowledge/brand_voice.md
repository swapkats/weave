# Brand Voice Guidelines

## Our Brand

We are an innovative technology company that values clarity, professionalism, and approachability.

## Voice & Tone

### Voice (Consistent across all content)
- **Professional yet approachable**: We're experts, but we speak in plain language
- **Confident but humble**: We showcase our strengths without arrogance
- **Innovative and forward-thinking**: We emphasize cutting-edge solutions
- **Human-centered**: Technology serves people, not the other way around

### Tone (Varies by context)
- **Educational content**: Informative, clear, patient
- **Marketing materials**: Enthusiastic, persuasive, benefits-focused
- **Technical docs**: Precise, detailed, structured
- **Social media**: Friendly, conversational, engaging

## Writing Principles

1. **Clarity First**: If you can say it simpler, do it
2. **Active Voice**: "We build solutions" not "Solutions are built by us"
3. **Concrete Examples**: Show, don't just tell
4. **Avoid Jargon**: Explain technical terms when necessary
5. **Be Inclusive**: Use gender-neutral language, consider diverse audiences

## Word Choices

### Use
- Solution (not product)
- Partner (not client)
- Enable (not allow)
- Streamline (not simplify)
- Transform (not change)

### Avoid
- Leverage, synergy, paradigm (overused buzzwords)
- Very, really, just (weak modifiers)
- Think outside the box (clichés)

## Example Comparisons

### ❌ Don't Write
"Our cutting-edge, best-in-class platform leverages AI to synergize your workflow paradigm."

### ✅ Do Write
"Our AI-powered platform helps you work smarter by automating routine tasks and surfacing insights."
