# Brand Voice Guide

## Our Brand Personality

We are **innovative, approachable, and trustworthy**.

### Voice Characteristics
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Professional** but not stuffy
- **Friendly** but not overly casual
- **Knowledgeable** but not condescending

## Writing Style

### Tone
- Use active voice
- Write in second person ("you") when addressing users
- Be conversational yet professional
- Show enthusiasm without exclamation marks!!!

### Language
- Use simple, clear language
- Avoid jargon unless necessary
- Explain technical terms when used
- Use contractions (we're, you'll) for friendliness

### Formatting
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Bullet points for lists
- Bold for emphasis, not italics
- Headers use sentence case

## Examples

### Good ✓
"Our AI-powered tools help you automate repetitive tasks, giving you more time to focus on what matters."

### Bad ✗
"Utilizing our cutting-edge AI-enabled technological solutions, enterprises can leverage automation paradigms to optimize workflow efficiencies."

## Words to Use
- Help, support, assist
- Simple, easy, straightforward
- Powerful, effective, reliable
- You, your, we, our

## Words to Avoid
- Leverage, utilize, optimize (overused buzzwords)
- Paradigm, synergy, holistic
- Revolutionary, game-changing (unless truly applicable)
- Obviously, clearly, simply (can sound condescending)
